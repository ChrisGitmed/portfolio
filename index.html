About me

Full-stack web developer with an affinity for sunsets.

My first experience with code was writing simple Python scripts to complete my General Ed at Santa Barbara City College. Something in me immediately saw the limitless possiblities that learning to write software could allow. I decided to take a few more Computer Science classes, and became more and more fascinated with technology and the people creating it. Eventually, while I loved learning at SBCC I was hungry for real world development experience. I quit my job, and enrolled in the Accelerated Web Development program at LearningFuze. I was finally able to spend all day, every day, becoming the best developer I can be. LearningFuze helped fill in the gaps in my education and taught me how to create full-stack, scalable applications from the ground up.

A lover of logic, algorithms, and clean code. When not at my computer, I'm frequently found lying by the beach, or adventuring in the mountains.
